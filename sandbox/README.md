# 🧪 The Sandbox — Your Experiment Lab!

This folder belongs to **Michael and Isabella**. It's your personal playground! 🎉

The files in `code_examples/` are the "textbook" — they stay clean so you can
always look back at a perfect example. But experimenting is how real programmers
learn, so when you want to change an example, try a wild idea, or break things
on purpose... **copy it here and go crazy!** 🚀

## How it works

- `sandbox/michael/` — Michael's experiments
- `sandbox/isabella/` — Isabella's experiments (make it whenever you're ready!)

## Rules of the lab

1. There are **no wrong answers** in the sandbox — errors are discoveries! 🔬
2. Name your files so future-you knows what they are (like `day1_name_tag_experiments.py`).
3. If you build something cool, show it off at Show and Tell! 🌟

Happy experimenting! 💻✨
